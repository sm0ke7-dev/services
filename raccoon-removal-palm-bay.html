Palm Bay residents are no strangers to wildlife encounters, but raccoons present unique challenges that require immediate attention. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I know if I have raccoons in my Palm Bay home?

Common signs include nocturnal sounds in attics or walls, visible roof damage, scattered garbage, distinctive five-toed paw prints, and strong odors from urine or feces. Raccoons are most active at night, so sounds typically occur after sunset.

Is raccoon removal safe for my family and pets?

Professional removal services prioritize safety through proper equipment, training, and protocols. We minimize risks to occupants and pets while ensuring humane treatment of removed animals. DIY removal attempts pose significantly greater risks to families.

What should I do if I find a raccoon in my attic?

Never attempt direct contact or removal yourself. Close off access to living areas, avoid loud noises that might panic the animal, and contact professional wildlife removal services immediately. Raccoons can become aggressive when cornered or protecting young.

‚ö†Ô∏è Prevention Tips for Palm Bay Homeowners

  • Secure garbage cans - Use tight-fitting lids and bungee cords
  • Remove food sources - Pet food, bird feeders, fallen fruit
  • Seal entry points - Regular inspection and maintenance
  • Trim vegetation - Keep branches away from rooflines
  • Install deterrents - Motion lights, sprinkler systems

How long does the raccoon removal process take?

Removal timelines vary depending on family group size, property complexity, and seasonal factors. Simple cases may resolve within days, while complex situations involving multiple animals or extensive damage might require several weeks for complete resolution.

Can I prevent raccoons from coming back?

Yes, through proper exclusion work, environmental modifications, and ongoing maintenance. Professional exclusion services provide long-term solutions when properly implemented and maintained by homeowners.
