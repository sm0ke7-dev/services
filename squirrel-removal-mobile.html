Signs You Need Mobile Squirrel Removal

Recognizing signs of squirrel infestation early can prevent extensive damage and reduce removal costs. When to call squirrel removal services depends on several key indicators that suggest active wildlife presence.

Auditory clues include scratching, scurrying, or chattering sounds in walls, attics, or ceiling spaces, particularly during early morning or evening hours when squirrels are most active. These sounds often intensify during breeding seasons when animals are more territorial.

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning Signs of Squirrel Activity

  • Scratching sounds - Noise in walls, attics, or ceilings
  • Visual sightings - Squirrels entering or exiting your property
  • Droppings evidence - Small dark pellets near nesting sites
  • Chewed materials - Damaged wiring, insulation, or structures
  • Entry holes - Damaged vents, screens, or roof areas

Visual evidence encompasses droppings, chewed materials, and entry holes. Squirrel droppings are typically small, dark pellets found near feeding areas or nesting sites. Chewed electrical wires, insulation, or wooden structures indicate active feeding behavior.

Exterior signs include damaged roof vents, torn screens, or holes in soffits and fascia boards. Squirrels often create multiple entry points, making thorough inspection essential for effective removal.

Property damage escalates quickly once squirrels establish nesting sites. They chew continuously to maintain tooth health, targeting electrical wiring, insulation, and structural components. This behavior creates fire hazards and compromises building integrity.
