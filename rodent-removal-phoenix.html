Signs of Rodent Infestation in Your Phoenix Home

Recognizing a rodent infestation Phoenix early can save you thousands in damage and protect your family's health. Common signs of rodents in house include scratching or scurrying sounds in walls or ceilings, particularly at night when these nocturnal pests are most active.

Physical evidence includes small, dark droppings near food sources, along baseboards, or in storage areas. You might notice gnaw marks on furniture, baseboards, or food packaging, along with greasy rub marks along walls where rodents regularly travel. An unusual ammonia-like odor often indicates a significant infestation.
