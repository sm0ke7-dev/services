Common Signs of Squirrel Infestation

Squirrel infestation signs typically begin with unusual sounds in your attic or walls. Squirrel noises attic dwellers commonly hear include:

  • Scratching and scurrying sounds, especially during early morning and late afternoon
  • Chattering and chittering noises from active squirrels
  • Thumping sounds from squirrels jumping or moving quickly
  • Gnawing sounds as they chew through materials

Physical evidence includes:

  • Droppings in attic spaces and secluded areas
  • Chewed holes around rooflines and entry points
  • Damaged insulation with visible nesting materials
  • Gnaw marks on wooden structures and wiring
  • Visible squirrels entering or exiting through roof areas
